Famous spells

Harry

Obliviate: The charm Gilderoy Lockhart uses to accidentally wipe his own memory, the big buffoon.
Sectumsempra: A mouthful of a curse invented by troubled teen Severus Snape.
Avada Kedavra: The Killing Curse is a tool of the Dark Arts and one of the three Unforgivable Curses.

Potter

Lumos: A charm that illuminates the tip of the caster's wand, allowing the caster to see in the dark.
Expelliarmus: Forces whatever an opponent is holding to fly out of their hand.
Wingardium Leviosa: Makes things levitate.

Spells

Alohomora: Unlocks objects such as doors or windows.
Accio: Summons an object towards the caster.
Expecto Patronum: Most powerful defensive charm known to wizardkind.
